My Experience Why Tech?

I am a London based junior developer currently undertaking a 12-week _nology coding bootcamp.

I am passionate about programming as it allows me to combine my creativity with logic to produce technological solutions to real-world problems.

I am excited to start my career in tech and to continue to grow and learn to create new and innovative content.
